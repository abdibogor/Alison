Bootstrap Grid
. Bootstrap works on a grid
. The grid has 12 Columns
. There are four grids
 	- One grid for eah screen size
	  . large (1200px and higher)
	  . Medium(992px-1200px)
	  . Small(768px-991px)
	  . Extra small(less than 768px)

Examples
. col-lg-6
	-Appliesto large(>1200px)
	-Takes up 6 columns

. col-md-4
	-Applies to medium(992px-1200px)
	-Takes up 4 columns

. col-sm-12
	- Applies to small (768px-991px)
	- Takes up 12 columns(the entire row)


Nesting Columns
. Create rows in a column
. Each row has 12 new columns

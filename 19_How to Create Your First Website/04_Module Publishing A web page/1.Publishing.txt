What Happens At Your Host???
. This is the only Potentially confusing bit...so listen up
. You need to know Exactly where at your host to upload your files to...
. Your host will have many Directories. You need to know exactly which Directory to upload into
. Most of the time - you will upload into a directory called: public_html

Publishing Explained...
. Ift it is not, you will need to email your host or call them and ask the following question...
"...which directory of yours am i supposed to upload my web pages to?"
. Normally, you will be told the following
. "public_html"
